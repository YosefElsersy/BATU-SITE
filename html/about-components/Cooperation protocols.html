COOPERATION PROTOCOLS

The Supreme Council of Colleges of Technology is responsible for the following:

-Developing the general policy for education and training in colleges

Technology in light of community needs. -Meeting the needs of the various sectors of the state for graduates of technical and vocational education.

-Raising the level of technical and vocational education and ensuring quality in colleges.

-Ensuring the management of colleges’ funding sources to achieve their goals.

-Approving the recommendations of the boards of trustees of technological colleges.

-Drawing a general policy for textbooks and study notes and setting their own systems.

- Establishing general controls for the relationship and method of distributing work among faculty members in colleges of technology.

-Determining the college admission rules and the numbers proposed for admission each year in light of the suggestions submitted by each college.

-Forming a technical committee to coordinate between the corresponding institutes and departments in the colleges of technology.

-Approving the internal regulations of technological colleges.

- Discussing and approving the report prepared by the head of the education sector at the end of each academic year on technical education in technological colleges, as well as the reports submitted by college directors.
